CNN-Based Skin Disease Detection

AI model for automatic classification of dermoscopic skin lesions.

Problem Statement

Manual dermatology diagnosis requires expert knowledge and is subjective. Our system builds an automated CNN-based classifier capable of identifying skin diseases from lesion images for early detection and improved healthcare accessibility.

Literature Review

Traditional ML systems depend on handcrafted features. Recent CNN architectures achieve strong performance but require large datasets.

Research Gap

Need lightweight CNN optimized for limited datasets and deployable for real-time classification.
