CNN-Based Skin Disease Detection System

AI model for automatic classification of skin lesion images

Problem Statement

Manual diagnosis of skin diseases requires dermatology expertise and may be subjective. This project builds an automated CNN-based image classifier for early detection.

Dataset

Mendeley Skin Disease Dataset containing 3230 dermoscopic images across 6 classes. Images resized to 128x128 and normalized.

Model Architecture

CNN with convolution layers, pooling layers, dropout, and dense layers using TensorFlow & Keras.

Results

Current validation accuracy: 46%
